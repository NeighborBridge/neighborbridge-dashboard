🎯 刁钻问题应对（实战）

Q1 Over-screening:
"How do you avoid over-screening?"
A: "We use a tiered approach. Initial screening is broad, but only a small subset moves forward. Most remain in behavioral support."
提示: 分层，不压系统
Q2 Physician burden:
"Does this increase PCP workload?"
A: "Most of the workflow is handled by CHWs and nurses. PCPs are only involved at key points."
提示: CHW驱动，医生减负
Q3 Early identification definition:
"How do you define early identification?"
A: "We focus on functional and behavioral changes before formal diagnosis."
提示: 功能变化，不是标签
Q4 Data:
"What data do you have?"
A: "We are early, but we are tracking screening volume and intervention uptake, building a real-world cohort."
提示: 不夸大，强调数据在形成
Q5 Rural scalability:
"Can this work in Montana?"
A: "The model relies on existing primary care and community resources, so it can adapt well to rural settings."
提示: 可复制
