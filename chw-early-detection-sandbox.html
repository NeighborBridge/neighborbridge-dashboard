CHW / Family Navigator Action List / 行动清单

✅ Allowed CHW Actions / 允许

  • provide family education handout
  • explain early signs in plain language
  • help caregiver prepare questions for PCP
  • remind family of referral / appointment
  • help coordinate school feedback
  • document non-clinical barriers
  • support follow-up completion
  • flag concern back to licensed clinician

  • diagnose FASD / ASD / ADHD
  • recommend medication
  • interpret lab / imaging / formal testing
  • provide psychotherapy
  • override physician plan
  • manage crisis independently

Fragmentation Points / 断裂点地图

1. Family sees behavior, school sees function, clinic sees symptoms — but no one sees the full pattern.

Payer-facing Value Logic / 支付方价值逻辑

This sandbox illustrates how earlier identification and navigation may help shift care from crisis-driven utilization toward earlier, lower-intensity support.

这个沙盒展示的是：早期识别与导航如何可能把服务从危机驱动型高成本利用，前移到更早、更低强度、更连续的支持。

Potential Value Signals / 潜在价值信号 (exploratory — not validated 探索性，非已验证)

• reduced fragmentation • earlier family support • improved follow-up completion • shorter time to intervention • fewer crisis escalations (to observe) • better cross-domain coordination

This is a hypothesis for exploratory collaboration — not a validated outcome or guaranteed cost reduction.

这是一个用于探索性合作的假设，不是已经验证的结果或成本减少保证。
