Tier 2a — William / 教会邻居型轻量支持

Role / 定位

Trusted nearby church deacon and neighbor; potential low-pressure practical / spiritual support, not emergency decision-maker.

可信任的教会 deacon 和近邻，可作为低压力的实际与属灵支持入口；不是 emergency decision-maker，也不是正式照护责任人。

Relational strategy / 关系策略

Use small, concrete, low-pressure requests rather than broad responsibility language.

不要一开始提出很重的照护责任，而是用小、具体、低压力的方式打开局面。

Suggested first step / 第一步建议

Invite William for a 15-20 minute brief visit or conversation to help Richard think through how to make mother feel welcomed and spiritually supported.

请 William 做一次 15-20 分钟 brief visit / conversation，帮助 Richard 一起想想如何让母亲感到被欢迎，并获得 practical and spiritual support。

Suggested language / 建议措辞

"William, I really appreciate your steadiness and faithfulness. I've been thinking about how to support my mom not only physically, but also spiritually. Since you live nearby and I trust your judgment, would you be willing sometime to stop by briefly, maybe just 15-20 minutes, and help me think through how to make her feel welcomed and spiritually supported?"

"William，我很感激你的稳重和忠心。我最近在想，除了身体照护之外，也希望给我妈妈一点属灵和实际上的支持。你住得近，我也信任你的判断。如果你愿意，能不能找个时候简单来坐 15-20 分钟，帮我一起想想怎样让她感到被欢迎、被支持？"

Guardrail / 边界

Do not frame this as asking William to take over caregiving. Frame it as a small shared discernment / support conversation.

不要把这件事说成让 William 接管照护；要说成一个小的、共同分辨和支持的 conversation。
