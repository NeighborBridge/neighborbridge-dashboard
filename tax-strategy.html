Strategy Layer

High Value

1. Home Office Optimization

• Square footage method vs. simplified method
• Deduct: Mortgage interest, utilities, repairs, depreciation
• Required: Exclusive and regular use for business

2. Accountable Plan (future S-corp ready)

• Reimbursements not taxable income
• Requirements: Business connection, substantiation, return of excess
• Future: S-corp shareholder-employee structure

3. Travel Structuring (multi-purpose trips)

• Business portion deductible, personal portion not
• Keep detailed logs: Date, purpose, attendees, outcomes
• Mix business with pleasure: Only business days deductible

4. Timing Strategy

• Accelerate expenses: Prepay subscriptions, stock up supplies
• Defer income: Invoice timing, payment terms
• Cash vs. accrual method optimization

5. Retirement

• Solo 401k: Up to $69,000 (2026)
• Backdoor Roth: No income limits, tax-free growth
• SEP IRA: Simpler, lower limits
