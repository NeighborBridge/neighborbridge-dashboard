Adoption Planning

Strategic framework for stakeholder adoption and implementation

Overview

Purpose: Define and coordinate the adoption strategy for NeighborBridge solutions across stakeholder groups.

Scope: End-to-end planning from initial engagement to full implementation.

Goal: Ensure seamless integration and maximize value realization for all partners.

Stakeholders & Roles

Executive Sponsors

C-level leadership providing strategic direction and resources

Clinical Champions

Healthcare providers driving clinical adoption and workflow integration

Technical Teams

IT and implementation specialists ensuring technical integration

End Users

Frontline staff and patients utilizing the solutions

Implementation Phases

Phase 1: Discovery & Assessment

Stakeholder interviews, needs analysis, and readiness assessment

Phase 2: Planning & Design

Solution design, workflow mapping, and implementation planning

Phase 3: Pilot Implementation

Limited rollout, testing, and feedback collection

Phase 4: Full Deployment

Organization-wide rollout, training, and support

Phase 5: Optimization & Scale

Continuous improvement, scaling, and value realization
