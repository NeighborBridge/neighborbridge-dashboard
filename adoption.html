Personal Family Formation & Adoption Readiness Dashboard

Long-term caregiving structure, foster-to-adopt exploration, and sustainable family formation planning — 长期照护结构、寄养转收养探索与可持续家庭建立规划

Current Status / 当前状态

Exploratory phased-entry stage — focus is caregiving capacity, not immediate adoption. 探索阶段，重点在照护能力，不在立即收养。

One Next Action / 当前行动

Strengthen backup caregiving structure and assess respite-foster readiness. 强化备份照护结构，评估 respite foster 准备度。

Current Constraint / 当前约束

Elder-care responsibility + limited backup coverage + career expansion may constrain caregiving bandwidth. 老人照护责任 + 备份覆盖有限 + 职业扩张可能制约照护带宽。

🧭 Core Philosophical Principle / 核心哲学原则

The goal is not simply "becoming a parent," but building a sustainable caregiving structure that can support attachment, stability, and continuity over time. 目标不只是"成为父亲"，而是建立可持续照护结构。

Long-term relational stability over rescue fantasy. 长期关系稳定性高于"拯救幻想"。This dashboard operates from sustainable systems-building, not emotional impulse or heroic narrative.

🏗️ Current Life Structure / 当前生活结构 Caregiving systems-capacity analysis

Not a deficit analysis — a caregiving systems-capacity analysis. 这不是缺陷分析，而是照护系统能力分析。

📋 Phased Family Formation Strategy / 分阶段家庭建立策略
Stage 1 — Respite Foster / Low-risk Entry 探索

Goals: test real caregiving bandwidth, build DSS trust, avoid premature irreversible commitment, strengthen backup network. 目标：测试真实照护带宽、建立 DSS 信任、避免过早不可逆承诺、强化支持网络。

Stage 2 — Focused Foster-to-Adopt Exploration 未来

Focus: legally free children preferred, realistic matching based on caregiving capacity, avoid overextension. 重点：优先法律自由儿童、基于照护能力的现实匹配、避免过度扩展。

Stage 3 — Long-term Adoption Decision 未来

Prerequisites: stable support network, sustainable workflow, caregiver redundancy, manageable elder-care overlap. 前提：稳定支持网络、可持续工作流程、照护者冗余、可控的老人照护叠加。

👥 Community-supported Parenting Structure / 社区支持式养育

The intended model is not isolated single-parent caregiving, but community-supported, relationship-based, operationally sustainable parenting. 目标不是孤立单亲照护，而是社区支持、关系为本、操作可持续的养育。

Abstract support is insufficient; named, reliable, operational support matters. 抽象支持不够，具体、可靠、可执行的支持才重要。
