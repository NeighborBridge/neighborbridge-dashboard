The goal is not simply "becoming a parent." The goal is building a sustainable long-term caregiving structure capable of supporting attachment, stability, and continuity over time.

Long-term relational stability over rescue fantasy. This dashboard operates from a framework of sustainable systems-building, not emotional impulse or heroic narrative.

长期关系稳定性高于"拯救幻想"。本面板基于可持续系统构建，而非情绪冲动或英雄叙事。

👥 Community-supported Parenting Structure / 社区支持式养育结构

The intended structure is not isolated single-parent caregiving. The intended structure is community-supported, relationship-based, and operationally sustainable.

目标结构不是孤立单亲照护，而是社区支持、关系为本、可操作可持续的结构。

Abstract support is insufficient. Named, reliable, operational support matters.

抽象支持是不够的。真正重要的是具体、可靠、可执行的支持结构。

⛪ Church relationships

🔄 Backup caregivers

🧘 Respite support
Regular, predictable, funded
🤝 Community integration
School, neighborhood, peer network
🔁 Practical redundancy
No single point of failure in caregiving
👴 Intergenerational support
Possible overlap or complement with elder care

🚩 Red Flags / Capacity Warnings / 预警信号

High moral commitment does not eliminate operational limits. 高度责任感不能消除 operational limits。

🔴 Red Flags that pause the pathway
  • Simultaneous elder-care crisis + child crisis
  • No respite structure in place
  • Caregiver isolation — no named backup
  • Unstable workflow / unresolved burnout
  • Inability to maintain continuity
  • No emotional sustainability plan
⚠️ Capacity warnings that require review
  • Overlap of elder-care crisis + foster transition
  • Lack of backup caregivers for both tiers
  • Career instability / major transition
  • Unrealistic optimism about bandwidth
  • Rushing timeline without infrastructure
  • Operating without community structure
