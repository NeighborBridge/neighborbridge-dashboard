Meaning: William Cox handover, Bayada/private-pay aide option, emergency flow card, and daily check-in structure may reduce single-point-of-failure risk.

2. Career and geographic stability

Source: Career / Faculty Pathway

Meaning: NC-based, flexible, outpatient/telehealth-compatible work structure supports long-term caregiving stability.

Boundary: Career expansion can also reduce bandwidth; flexibility must be protected intentionally.

3. Community-supported parenting

Source: Stakeholders / Church / Community network

Meaning: Abstract community support must become named, reliable, operational backup support.

Boundary: "Supportive community" is not sufficient unless specific people, roles, availability, and limits are identified.

4. Clinical background

Source: FASD / ADHD / pediatric expertise

Meaning: Richard's background can help him understand trauma, neurodevelopment, school systems, and caregiver stress.

Boundary: Clinical expertise is an asset, not a readiness guarantee. It must be paired with humility, role boundaries, respite, and support.
