## What This Means for Decision Making

### Not changing
- The strategic direction is sound — FQHC/govt consulting as primary revenue stream, with academic legitimacy as the enabler
- Faculty pathway exploration is worth continuing
- Pursuing quality content (Finding 2, white papers) is valuable even without immediate monetization

### What changes
- **Budget expectations**: If any spending depends on the original revenue timeline, push assumptions out 6–12 months
- **Funding model**: If possible, identify non-revenue funding (small grants, speaking fees, consulting retainers) to bridge the gap between "no revenue" and "first paid contract"
- **Part of a larger org**: If Richard is considering joining an existing center/institute/group, the income bridge may be shorter than going fully independent
- **Stakeholder communication**: If anyone was told to expect results by Aug 2026, adjust expectations now (including internal—Richard's own timeline expectations)
