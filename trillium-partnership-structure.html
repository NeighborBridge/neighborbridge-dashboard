🧭 当前战略问题

核心问题

Richard 如何主导 clinical logic、implementation design、FASD pathway 和 academic narrative，同时让 Holly 提供本地 public health legitimacy、community credibility 和 relationship bridge？

核心判断：

核心问题不是"谁拥有项目"，而是如何形成一个稳妥的 Trillium-facing partnership structure。

🎯 角色边界

按领域划分

👤 Richard 主导

  • clinical logic
  • implementation design
  • FASD early identification pathway
  • pilot structure
  • measurement
  • academic narrative
  • payer-facing value logic

🤝 Holly 提供

  • local public health legitimacy
  • FASD community credibility
  • relationship bridge
  • funding landscape insight
  • local trust
  • public health alignment

Preferred external framing:

local public health partnership + clinical / implementation leadership

⛔ 不该做什么

边界
🚫 不让 Holly 变成 project owner
🚫 不把 Richard 放成 Holly 的 assistant
🚫 不让 Richard 显得单打独斗
⚠️ 不在结构未清楚前谈太具体 pilot budget
⚠️ 不过早暴露 NeighborBridge 的完整 pathway / workflow IP
⚠️ 不把 Trillium conversation 变成 sales pitch
⚠️ 不在 partner interest 还不明确时承诺 formal pilot

📢 对外表述原则

统一措辞

❌ 非

Holly 主导 / Richard 协助

❌ 非

Richard 单打独斗

✅ 优选框架

local public health partnership + clinical / implementation leadership

英文对外建议

Richard leads the clinical logic, implementation design, pathway structure, and measurement framework. Holly provides local public health credibility, community legitimacy, and guidance on how this work may fit within North Carolina's FASD and public health landscape.

中文对应

Richard 主导临床逻辑、实施设计、路径结构和评估框架；Holly 提供本地公共卫生可信度、社区合法性，以及关于该工作如何嵌入北卡 FASD 与公共卫生格局的判断。

💬 给 Holly 的轻量对齐问题

建议提问

英文：

"Given your understanding of the North Carolina FASD and public health landscape, where do you think this work would be most naturally positioned — as clinical education, early identification pathway development, or a small exploratory collaboration with Trillium?"

中文理解：

"从你对北卡 FASD 和公共卫生格局的了解来看，你觉得这个工作最自然的定位是什么？是临床教育、早期识别路径建设，还是与 Trillium 的小范围探索性合作？"

📋 进入下一步的触发条件

Decision triggers

只有在以下情况出现后，才推进到更具体 pilot / funding / formal brief：

Trillium asks for more detail
Holly confirms she is comfortable being a local legitimacy / relationship bridge
Cindy or another Trillium leader asks about next step
A specific FQHC site becomes plausible
There is an opportunity to discuss exploratory learning phase rather than formal pilot

⚠️ 风险提醒

需持续关注

IP risk: 不提前展示完整 NeighborBridge workflow / proprietary pathway

Role confusion risk: 不让 Holly 或 Trillium 误解 ownership

Sales pitch risk: 不把 conversation 变成 product pitch

Funding timing risk: 资金构想未成熟前，不主动提出具体 budget

Contracting risk: 如涉及 speaker reimbursement 或 vendor contract，需另行检查 IP / insurance / audit 条款

📌 下一步

行动项
与 Holly 做一次轻量角色对齐
保持 Trillium 主线为 education / exploration / early identification pathway
等 Trillium 明确兴趣后，再准备 formal brief
如需与 Cindy follow-up，先用 general update，不直接抛完整 pilot budget
在 Trillium 主 dashboard 中保留 payer strategy，不把 partnership governance 内容混在主页面
